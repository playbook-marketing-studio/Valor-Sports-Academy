Coach Corey Bibe

Coach & Co-Founder · Two-time conference champion linebacker · ISSA certified

Coach & Co-Founder

Built in the weight room

Corey's relationship with training started where it does for a lot of athletes: on the football field. Growing up playing the game, he learned fast that what happened in the gym showed up on the field, and that realization turned into a lifelong passion for training.

He took it to Southwestern Kansas, where he played linebacker and became a two-time conference champion. Competing at that level taught him what championship culture actually looks like, and it's the standard he brings to Valor every day. After earning his ISSA certification, he teamed up with his brother to build the academy, combining family, passion, and a shared vision for the next generation.

His philosophy is simple: give back. He believes training should be engaging and fun while building the discipline that pays off in the classroom, the workplace, and life. Every athlete who walks in isn't just training their body. They're building character.
