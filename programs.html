Camp logistics

What parents ask before camp

What should my athlete bring?

Athletic clothes, training shoes (and cleats for football sessions), and a labeled water bottle. We provide all training equipment. Dress for the weather, since some work is outdoors.

What are drop-off and pick-up like?

Drop off a few minutes before the start time and pick up at the listed end time at our Richland facility, 1973 Fowler Street. A coach is on hand at the start and end of every session.

What's your refund or cancellation policy?

Reach out before your registered week starts and we'll work with you on a credit or refund. Life happens, just let us know as early as you can. Call 509-987-4612 with any scheduling questions.

Do you serve families from Kennewick and Pasco?

Yes. Our camps run in Richland and we welcome athletes from across the Tri-Cities, including Kennewick, Pasco, and West Richland.
