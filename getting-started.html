Playbook Marketing Studio/Onboarding

What we need to get started

A short kickoff checklist for the Valor engagement. Two access handoffs, one decision on the giveaway, and we're live.

Step 1 · Access

Hand us the keys

We can't move the needle on a site or ad account we can't reach. These two take about ten minutes each.

Website access

So we can rebuild & manage the site

Add us as a contributor on the current Wix site, or hand over the login. If we move off Wix, we'll also want the domain registrar.

  • Wix site collaborator invite (or login) for valorsportsacademywa.com
  • Domain registrar login (where the .com was purchased) so we can point DNS if needed
  • Any existing logo files, photos, and video you have in full resolution

Meta access

So we can run ads & the giveaway

Everything runs through Meta Business Manager. Assign Playbook as a partner so the assets stay in your name, not ours.

  • Facebook Page admin access for Valor Sports Academy
  • Instagram account login or linked access
  • Meta Business Manager partner access (or we'll help you create one)
  • Ad account + a payment method on file for ad spend

Step 2 · The giveaway

Pick the prize that prints money

A giveaway's real job isn't the prize. It's collecting a list of parents who'd actually pay you, then converting them. So we want a prize that attracts buyers, not freebie-hunters, and that walks the winner straight into your highest-value service.

Recommended

Summer of Valor

~$300 perceived value

One free week of camp + a 1-on-1 assessment + two personal training sessions. Attracts exactly the parent who books camps, and the winner experiences personal training, which is the upsell.

Best revenue path. Seeds your highest-margin service.

Free Week of Camp

$90 value

One week of Summer Development. Simple, on-brand, low hard cost since the class is already running. Pulls camp-intent parents.

Strong & simple. Easiest to fulfill.

Personal Training Pack

~$240 value

Four 1-on-1 sessions. Highest perceived value and pushes your best per-athlete revenue stream, but narrower appeal than camp.

Premium pull. Great for a PT push.

The real money

Every entrant is a lead. One athlete wins, but everyone who entered gets a "$10 off your first week" code and a follow-up. One giveaway with 150 entries and a 10% convert at $90/week is more revenue than the prize ever costs. That's why we run it through a Meta lead form or a simple landing page that captures name, email, phone, sport, and the kid's age.
