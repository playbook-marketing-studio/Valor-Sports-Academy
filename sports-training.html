Common questions

Speed training, answered

What age should a kid start speed and agility training?

Around age 8 is a great starting point. Early training focuses on coordination, movement quality, and fun; as athletes mature we add acceleration mechanics and strength. Read the full guide →

Does speed and agility training actually work?

Yes. Speed is a trainable skill. Acceleration mechanics, footwork, and proper deceleration all improve with coaching and consistent reps. Read the full guide →

How often should my athlete train?

Two to three focused sessions a week is the sweet spot for most youth athletes, with rest to recover and adapt. Read the full guide →

Do you train athletes from Kennewick and Pasco?

Yes. We're in Richland at 1973 Fowler Street and train athletes from across the Tri-Cities, including Kennewick, Pasco, and West Richland.
