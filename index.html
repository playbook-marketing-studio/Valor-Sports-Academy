Common questions

What Tri-Cities parents ask us

What ages does Valor train?

We train youth athletes ages 8 to 18 across every sport, plus adults one-on-one. Classes are grouped by age so athletes train with their peers.

Does my child need football experience to start?

No. Most athletes start with our all-sport Sports Training to build strength, speed, and conditioning. Football Training is position-specific for kids who want to focus on the game.

What age should a kid start speed and agility training?

Most kids are ready around age 8, once they can follow coaching cues and move through basic drills safely. We scale every session to the athlete in front of us.

How big are the classes?

Every class is capped at 25 athletes with three coaches per session, so your athlete gets real attention instead of getting lost in a crowd.

Where are you located and who do you serve?

We train at 1973 Fowler Street in Richland, WA and work with families across the Tri-Cities, including Kennewick, Pasco, and West Richland.

How much does training cost?

Summer camps are $90 per week for Summer Development and $75 per week for Football Skills, with a $10 discount for siblings or a second camp. Adult personal training is quoted to your goals.

How do I get started?

Book a free assessment. We'll show you where your athlete stands today and map the plan to move them forward.
