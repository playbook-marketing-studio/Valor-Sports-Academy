Common questions

Youth football, answered

Does my child need football experience to start?

No. We coach first-time players and varsity athletes side by side, grouped by age. Beginners learn stance, footwork, and fundamentals while experienced players sharpen position technique and football IQ.

What ages do you train for football?

Ages 8 to 18, from elementary through high school, grouped so athletes train with their peers.

Do you offer youth football training near Kennewick and Pasco?

Yes. Our facility is in Richland at 1973 Fowler Street, and we train athletes from across the Tri-Cities including Kennewick, Pasco, and West Richland.

What positions do you coach?

All of them. Our coaches have coached defensive backs, quarterbacks, and linebackers at the high school and college level, and train technique for every position.
